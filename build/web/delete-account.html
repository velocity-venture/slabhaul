SlabHaul Account Deletion Request

We're sorry to see you go. You can request deletion of your SlabHaul account and all associated data by contacting us directly.

To delete your account, email us at:
privacy@velocityventureholdings.com

Use the subject line: Account Deletion Request
Include the email address associated with your SlabHaul account.

What gets deleted

  • Your account and login credentials
  • Your trip logs and catch records
  • Your saved favorites and calculator presets
  • Your personal profile information

What may be retained

  • Anonymized bait reports (with all personal identifiers removed) may be retained to improve fishing recommendations for the community

Timeline

Account deletion requests are processed within 30 days of receiving your email.
