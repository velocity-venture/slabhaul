SlabHaul Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 1, 2026 | Last Updated: March 1, 2026

1. Who We Are

SlabHaul is developed by Velocity Venture Holdings LLC. This policy applies to the SlabHaul mobile app on Android and iOS.

2. Information We Collect

We collect email address and display name when you register, catch reports and trip logs you submit, bait reports with optional GPS coordinates, calculator presets and favorites, and device location only when you use map features.

3. How We Use Your Information

To provide the SlabHaul experience, display nearby lakes and conditions, save your data across devices, power community bait reports, and diagnose app issues.

4. Location Data

Location is accessed only while you actively use the app. We do not track location in the background. Location in bait reports is optional.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. We use Supabase for secure data storage, and public APIs (Open-Meteo, USGS, NOAA) for weather and water data. No personal data is shared with these services.

6. Data Retention

Your data is retained while your account is active. Account deletion removes personal data within 30 days.

7. Your Rights

You may access, correct, or delete your data at any time. You may use guest mode without an account. You may revoke location access via device settings.

8. Children's Privacy

SlabHaul is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

9. Security

We use HTTPS/TLS encryption, row-level database security, and OAuth 2.0 authentication.

10. Changes

We may update this policy. Continued use constitutes acceptance of changes.
